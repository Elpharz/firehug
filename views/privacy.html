Privacy Policy

The Festival app is designed to enhance your Festival experience. The four features are: a schedule of events, information for getting around, a Healthy Dialog breakfast activity that assigns Mozillians to groups each day, and a 3 Questions response form to measure the pulse of the collective Festival experience.

We care about your privacy and you should know the following:

If you choose to respond to the optional 3 Questions response form, your responses will be collected by Mozilla and used as described in this notice.

Your list of positive impact people and Healthy Dialog groupings will be stored with your mozillians.org username so that it can be made available to you after the Festival.

Mozilla will use your responses to analyze and share aggregate connections amongst Mozillians. You can request deletion of this data at any time by contacting Festival2013-app@mozilla.com.

This app is integrated with Persona and mozillians.org, our community directory. Their privacy policies apply and can be found at: http://www.mozilla.org/persona/privacy-policy/ http://mozilla.org/privacy-policy.html
